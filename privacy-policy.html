Privacy Policy

Vers Libre · Last updated: February 2026

Vers Libre is a volunteer-run, artist-led radio and sound collective based in Bergen, Norway. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you interact with our services, including our website, social media pages, and radio programming.

Information We Collect

We may collect basic contact information (such as your name and email address) when you voluntarily reach out to us via email, social media, or our website. We do not collect any information without your knowledge or consent.

How We Use Your Information

Any information you provide is used solely to respond to your enquiries, coordinate programming and events, or communicate relevant news about Vers Libre. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for commercial purposes.

Social Media

When you message us via Facebook or Instagram, those platforms' own privacy policies also apply. We use these channels only to communicate with our community and do not store your messages beyond what is necessary to respond.

Data Retention

We retain contact information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@verslib.re.
